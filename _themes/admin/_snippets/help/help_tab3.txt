<h3>Background Pictures: How do I Choose the Background Pictures behind my {fp:Group} Page?</h3>
<p>
You can choose the set of pictures that appear in the background of your {fp:gallery} pages (when you are using a theme that shows background pictures). You do not need to "hide" the {fp:project}...if it is a "Slide Carousel", it will not appear in the lists of {fp:projects}.
</p>
<ol>
	<li>
		Make a new {fp:project}. Click on the {fp:projects} tab, then the "New {fp:Projects}" button. Call it "slideshow" or something like that.
	</li>
	<li>
		Click on , "Show/Hide Advanced Features". Look for the checkbox called, "Slide Carousel", about the seventh item on the page. Click on the box to turn it on.
	<li>
		Click "Save". 
	</li>

</ol>
